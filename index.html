Who am I?

“The craft of programming begins with empathy, not formatting or languages or tools or algorithms or data structures.” – Kent Beck.

Hello there! My name is Omar Elemary I am a university student studying Software Engineering and Embedded Systems at McMaster University!

My journey into the world of software development began when I first realized I wanted to be an engineer.

From childhood, I aspired to be a pioneer of technology similar to Bill Gates, Steve Jobs, and Elon Musk. Just like those legends, I wanted to create; I wanted to innovate; but more importantly; I wanted to help.

My hobbies always centered around technology as I am deeply infatuated with all aspects of it such as its intricate yet intuitive nature. But nothing has instantly attracted me more than software development. It allowed me to exercise my problem solving and creative muscles but more importantly; it opened up the doors for me to fulfil my true goal. For that I travelled all the way from Saskatoon, SK to pursue it and now here I am!

The limitless potential of programming stems not just from its many applications, rather from the many ways it can change people’s lives for the better. For that I continue to learn, experience and expand my arsenal of tools in hopes of taking those first steps towards a new future and I invite YoU to join me in my journey as well. Together we can reshape the landscape of software and embrace its power to spark a technological revolution.

Programming

The perfect blend of creativity, problem solving and fun.I began my programming journey in highschool where I created simple Guido Van Robot and Python turtle programs. From the moment I created my first Hello World! program, I instantly fell in love. Its challenges drive me; Its flexibility intrigues me; Its endless possiblities excite me; This is what I am meant to do!
